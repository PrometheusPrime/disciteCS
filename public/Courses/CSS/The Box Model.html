The Box Model

All HTML elements are rendered as Rectangular boxes.

<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
  <head>
    <title>Intro To CSS</title>
  </head>

  <body>
    <h1>Hello World</h1>
  </body>
</html>

The Content is in the middle surrounded by Padding followed by the Border and then the Margin.

Padding is the space between the content and the Border. Margin is outside the Border, it is the space between Elements.

The Margin, Border and Padding are divided into four parts; top, bottom, left and right.
